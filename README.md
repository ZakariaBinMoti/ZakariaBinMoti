## 💼 Case Studies

<details>
<summary><b>🚀 Store Performance Upgrade — 4.5s → 1.9s Load Time</b></summary>
<br/>

> **Challenge:** A Shopify store with slow load times leading to high bounce rates and lost revenue.

**Actions Taken:**
- Audited and eliminated render-blocking scripts
- Implemented lazy loading for images and third-party widgets
- Optimized Liquid templates and reduced DOM complexity
- Enabled CDN caching and asset compression

**Results:**
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Load Time | 4.5s | ⚡ 1.9s |
| Bounce Rate | High | 📉 Reduced |
| Engagement | Low | 📈 Increased |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🎯 Conversion Rate Optimization — Product Page Redesign</b></summary>
<br/>

> **Challenge:** Low add-to-cart rates despite healthy traffic.

**Actions Taken:**
- Redesigned product page hierarchy and visual flow
- Added social proof, trust badges & urgency signals
- Streamlined checkout CTA placement
- Improved mobile UX and thumb-zone interactions

**Results:**
- 📈 Higher conversion rate
- 🛒 More add-to-carts
- ⭐ Better customer trust signals

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>⚙️ Custom Multi-Location Inventory System</b></summary>
<br/>

> **Challenge:** Complex inventory logic across multiple warehouses with custom product selection flows.

**Actions Taken:**
- Built custom Shopify app with metafield-driven logic
- Created advanced product bundle and variant selection flow
- Integrated with fulfillment API for real-time stock sync

**Results:**
- ✅ Zero overselling incidents
- 📦 Automated fulfillment routing
- 🔧 Fully maintainable by store team

</details>
